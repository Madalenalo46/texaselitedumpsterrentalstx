Frequently Asked Questions

Is it worth renting a dumpster just for leaves?

If you have more than a handful of trees, yes. It will save you hours of bagging and hauling. It's the ultimate weekend-reclaiming tool.

Is renting a dumpster complicated?

It's the easiest part of the whole process. You call. We talk about the size of your enemy. We deliver your weapon. You win the war.

What if I fill it up and I’m not done yet?

This is common in big cleanups. Just call us. We can bring you an empty dumpster and haul the full one away. It's called a swap-out, and we can do it quickly to keep you moving.

Is a temporary roll off rental really cheaper than a permanent bin?

If you only have a major cleanout a few times a year, then yes, almost always. You only pay for the dumpster service when you actually need the high capacity.

How do we protect our parking lot or loading dock area?

We always recommend you place down sheets of plywood where the dumpster's steel wheels will rest. It’s a simple, professional step that prevents any scuffs or scratches on the pavement.

West Jordan, UT Insights: Population,
Zip Codes, Influence, and Service Areas

West Jordan is a city in Salt Lake County, Utah, United States. It is a suburb of Salt Lake City. According to the 2020 Census, the city had a population of 116,961, placing it as the third most populous in the state. The city occupies the southwest end of the Salt Lake Valley at an elevation of 4,330 feet (1,320 m). Named after the nearby Jordan River, the limits of the city begin on the river's western bank and end in the eastern foothills of the Oquirrh Mountains, where Kennecott Copper Mine, the world's largest man-made excavation, is located.
